Our Story

A Decade of Quiet Mornings

Noir & Bloom began as a single roaster in the back of a flower shop — hence the name. What started as coffee for friends became a neighborhood ritual, then a small chain, but the intention never changed: slow down, taste carefully, and treat every cup like it matters.

Today our roasters and baristas still work the same way — small batches, honest sourcing, and a counter that feels more like a living room than a storefront.
